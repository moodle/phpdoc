file_picker::__construct ( stdClass $options)

Constructs a file picker object.

The following are possible options for the filepicker:

  • accepted_types (*)
  • return_types (FILE_INTERNAL)
  • env (filepicker)
  • client_id (uniqid)
  • itemid (0)
  • maxbytes (-1)
  • maxfiles (1)
  • buttonname (false)
