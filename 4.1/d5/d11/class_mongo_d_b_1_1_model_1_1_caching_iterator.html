MongoDB\Model\CachingIterator::__construct ( Traversable $traversable)

Initialize the iterator and stores the first item in the cache.

This effectively rewinds the Traversable and the wrapping IteratorIterator. Additionally, this mimics behavior of the SPL iterators and allows users to omit an explicit call to rewind() before using the other methods.
